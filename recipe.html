Non-Alcoholic Piña Colada

Author: Mary Stone

Published: March 10, 2024

This non-alcoholic piña colada is everyone's favorite!

Cuisine: American

Prep Time: 1 minute

Cook Time: 2 minutes

Total Time: 3 minutes

Servings: 4 servings

Category: Drink

Calories: 120 calories

Ingredients

  • 400ml of pineapple juice
  • 100ml cream of coconut
  • Ice

Instructions

  1. Blend: Blend 400ml of pineapple juice and 100ml cream of coconut until smooth.
  2. Fill: Fill a glass with ice.
  3. Pour: Pour the pineapple juice and coconut mixture over ice.
